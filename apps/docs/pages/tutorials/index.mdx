# Tutorials

The [Quickstart](/quickstart) pages are the shortest path to a
finding. Tutorials are the long form &mdash; opinionated, end-to-end
walkthroughs that finish with a runnable artefact (a CI workflow, a
customer-ready bundle, a model A/B comparator). Each tutorial declares
its inputs, its time budget, and the deliverable it produces.

## How to read a tutorial

Every tutorial is structured the same way:

1. **Scenario** &mdash; the realistic situation the tutorial models.
2. **Inputs you need** &mdash; URLs, tokens, repos, models.
3. **Time budget** &mdash; what you spend on each phase.
4. **Steps** &mdash; copy-paste-runnable.
5. **Deliverable** &mdash; the artefact you ship to a customer, an
   auditor, your CI/CD, or your future self.

Tutorials assume the [Installation](/getting-started/installation)
and [first scan](/getting-started/first-scan) pages are already
complete &mdash; they don&rsquo;t re-explain the basics.
